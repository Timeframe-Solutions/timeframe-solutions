### FAQ items (8)

**What's included in KES 80,000?**

> Up to 6 pages, mobile-first responsive design, WhatsApp integration, SEO setup (meta tags, sitemap, OG tags), a contact/inquiry form, Google Maps embed, Vercel deployment, domain connection, and 2 rounds of revisions.

**How long does a project take?**

> Most projects are done in 2–3 weeks from the day you confirm and pay the deposit. That timeline assumes you provide your content (text, photos, logo) within the first few days.

**Do I need to buy hosting?**

> No. We deploy on Vercel — the free tier is more than enough for a business site. You only need to pay for your domain name (typically KES 1,000–2,000/year from a registrar like Truehost Kenya).

**Can I update the website myself?**

> For small text changes, yes — we'll walk you through it. For structural changes, we offer a monthly maintenance retainer at KES 5,000–10,000/month depending on volume.

**Do you integrate M-Pesa?**

> Yes, but Daraja integration (M-Pesa STK Push, paybill, etc.) is scoped and priced separately — it's a backend service that adds complexity beyond a standard business site. Send us a WhatsApp and we'll give you a quote.

**What if I need more than 6 pages?**

> Additional pages beyond the 6-page package are quoted at KES 8,000–12,000 per page depending on complexity. We'll confirm the scope before we start.

**Do you work with businesses outside Nairobi?**

> Yes. We work with any business in Kenya or East Africa. Most communication happens over WhatsApp and email — no in-person meetings required.

**How do I pay?**

> 50% upfront before work begins, 50% on delivery and your approval. We accept M-Pesa (Paybill or Till) and bank transfer.
